Crystal Collector

Here's how you play the game

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

By clicking on one of the four crystals below you will add a specific amount of points to your score.

The point og the game is to your score to the random number.

If your score goes over the number generated you will be alert will pop up letting you know you have lost.

The value each crystal is randomly generated each round and wont be shown so keep a mental note of their value.
